2. 【Instinct】The sound of burp：geu（嗝）

>> Cause
>>

In the **era of gathering**, the ancients relied on **fruit** as their staple food. Because fruit contains more gas, eating too much can easily lead to bloating. Naturally cause **burp**s and produce sounds "**geu**".

## Derived concepts

### 嗝（burp）

>> Concept carries causality: sound-carry-action

Directly carry this behavior concept in the way that the behavior produces sound.

>> Practical expression

1. [wu] geu a geu(嗝啊嗝) 
   1. [mandarin]

   1. Translated word by word
      1. qye（吃）- eat
      2. dao（到）- until；reaching
      3. geu（嗝）- burp
      4. a（啊）-  Interjection which has the meaning of opening mouth
   2. Translated as a whole: eat until burp
